What is the Caminho de Santiago?

The Caminho de Santiago is a legendary pilgrimage route that leads to the shrine of the apostle Saint James in Santiago de Compostela, Spain. With origins dating back over 1,000 years, it offers a deeply spiritual and transformative journey across scenic trails in Spain, Portugal, and beyond.

Essentials Along the Way

Here are a few key tips to help you stay safe, healthy, and mindful throughout your journey on the Caminho de Santiago.

Stay Hydrated

Carry a reusable water bottle and refill whenever possible. Staying hydrated is crucial on long walking days.

Foot Care

Use proper footwear and care for your feet daily—blister prevention is your best friend on the trail.

Sun Protection

Wear sunscreen, sunglasses, and a hat. Shade is rare in some stretches, so prepare accordingly.

Reflect & Journal

Take a few moments each evening to write down your thoughts or learnings—it adds meaning to your path.
